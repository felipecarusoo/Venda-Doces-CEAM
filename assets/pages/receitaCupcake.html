Cupcake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Place the butter in the mixer and beat until creamy.
  2. Gradually add sugar with the mixer on until it becomes a fluffy mixture.
  3. Add the eggs one by one while continuously beating.
  4. Remove the mixture from the mixer and add sifted flour, alternating with milk.
  5. Add vanilla extract and, finally, the baking powder. Mix gently.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  7. Place the batter in the cupcake molds.
  8. Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes.
  9. Fill and top with your favorite frosting.
