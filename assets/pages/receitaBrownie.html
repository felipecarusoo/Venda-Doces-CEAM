Brownie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 350g dark chocolate
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups sugar
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Grease a rectangular pan approximately 23x33 cm in diameter.
  3. Cover with parchment paper, grease the paper with butter, and dust with flour.
  4. Place 250 grams of dark chocolate and butter in a double boiler.
  5. Let it melt until smooth.
  6. In a mixer, combine eggs, vanilla, all-purpose flour, a pinch of salt, and sugar.
  7. Beat on high speed for 10 minutes.
  8. Reduce the speed and gradually add the melted chocolate.
  9. Add the remaining chopped dark chocolate.
  10. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
  12. Remove from the oven when the edges of the brownie start to pull away from the sides.
  13. The center will still be very soft, but it will harden as it cools.
