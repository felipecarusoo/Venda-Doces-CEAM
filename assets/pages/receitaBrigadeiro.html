Brigadeiro Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, mix all the ingredients.
  2. Heat on low, stirring constantly until it comes off the bottom of the pan.
  3. Turn off the heat and continue stirring until the mixture cools.
  4. With buttered hands, form small balls and roll them in sprinkles.
  5. Place the balls in paper molds.

May our sweetness be contagious!
